Project Overview

Supply Chain Visibility

Developed a comprehensive Tableau dashboard that provides real-time visibility into supply chain operations, enabling data-driven decision making and proactive problem resolution.

The dashboard integrates multiple data sources to create a unified view of inventory levels, supplier performance, delivery timelines, and cost metrics across the entire supply chain network.

Dashboard Features

Performance Metrics

Real-time tracking of key performance indicators including on-time delivery, inventory turnover, and supplier reliability.

Historical data analysis and trend identification to predict future supply chain challenges and opportunities.

Automated alerts for critical issues like stockouts, delayed shipments, and quality concerns.
